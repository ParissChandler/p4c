How To

"Prepare4Conversation" is a non-profit website that its mission is helping anyone in need to organize thoughts in a simple and interactive way and get ready for a conversation. It to helps achieve a better process for dealing with differences between you and the other side.

This website contains universal questions that can be applied to prepare for any conversation (family, boss, friends, etc.) that one tends to avoid. To get ready, you simply need to follow the questions and fill in the answers. You are the only one who can view your answers. Think about what you want from the other side, what they want from you. Is it fair?

When you're done, you will be able to get a summary of the preparation you wrote by sending it to your email. During the process of getting ready for a preparation, you will be able to return to your current preparation and edit it. You can enter your preparation and change it multiple times as long as you do not start a new preparation or send the final preparation to your email.

In a conversation of more than two partcipants, you will have to fill the prep sheet separately for each counterpart you will be preparing to speak to.

Keep in mind 3 things:

  1. This website will help you prepare for a conversation, not to make decisions.
  2. This website is against verbal or physical violence of any kind.
  3. This is not legal advice.

Good luck!
